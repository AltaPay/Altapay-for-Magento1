AltaPay and Magento plug-in

AltaPay has made it much easier for you as merchant/developer to receive secure payments in your Magento web shop. AltaPay is fully integrated with Magento via a plug-in. All you have to do is to install the plug-in, which will only take a few minutes to complete.
